Real World Judo League

Welcome

The RWJL is a web project to allow Judo players from all over the world, of all levels, to compete against one another!

The site will allow you to find other Judo players and arrange a fight with them, you will be able to record who wins and move up (or down) in the RWJL rankings based on how you do.

The plan is to have a wide variety of "ladders" for clubs, areas, nations, international, ages, whatever! With your support the hope is to start having prizes for the winners of ladders!

This site is firmly aimed at the 95% of Judoka who do Judo socially and not at the elite level, it is hoped that the RWJL will give us all people of equal ability to compete against.
